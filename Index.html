The Price of Fame

Escape the Struggle, Embrace Redemption

Themes

The book explores themes of survival, friendship, betrayal, and redemption.

Main Characters

  • Jamal "Jay" Carter: The rising rapper trying to escape his troubled past.
  • Kareem "Reem" Johnson: Jay's childhood best friend, torn between loyalty and survival.
  • Whitney Jackson: Jay's first love, whose betrayal changed his outlook on relationships.
  • Uncle Dereck: A former street hustler who tries to pull Jay out of the game.

Excerpts
